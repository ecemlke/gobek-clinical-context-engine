🌐 Layer 6: Clinical Safety Network

This is not a traditional marketplace. It is a distributed safety infrastructure layer designed to maintain Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) integrity at scale by connecting AI triage directly to specialized, verified practitioners.

🛡️ Scaling HITL Integrity

To safely scale AI in clinical nutrition, absolute medical oversight must scale with it. The Clinical Network allows the AI's triage system to automatically route complex cases (or user-requested reviews) to a live network of practitioners based on specialty (e.g., matching a Crohn's patient to an IBD-certified RD). This ensures the "Human-in-the-Loop" is always available, accountable, and specialized.
